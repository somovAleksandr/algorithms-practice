# Matrix Row Swapper

A simple Python program that:

- Generates a 6×6 matrix with random integers from 0 to 10,
- Prints the original matrix,
- Creates a new matrix where rows are swapped in pairs:
  - 1st ↔ 2nd
  - 3rd ↔ 4th
  - 5th ↔ 6th
- Prints the result.

The original matrix remains **unchanged** — the function returns a new matrix.

## Example Output

Original Matrix:
7 3 9 2 1 5
4 8 1 6 7 2
9 0 5 3 4 1
2 6 7 8 9 0
1 4 3 5 2 6
7 8 9 10 11 12

Swapped Matrix:
4 8 1 6 7 2
7 3 9 2 1 5
2 6 7 8 9 0
9 0 5 3 4 1
7 8 9 10 11 12
1 4 3 5 2 6
